Dear Prince,

Loving you is easy,it comes with the winds
wraps me with a blanket,soft and warm to the touch
Without realising I've made a home out of you,
musky cologne and big doe eyes
wavy hair and soft hands to hold,all mine
I love you without knowing how ,or when or from where
maybe when we walked through the roads of Banaras
or when you'll shyly smile on seeing me everytime
I love you like this because I don't know any other way to love,
and to keep loving you whichever way you want to be loved
I'll learn to weave you grass rings,
for all ten of your fingers
you can tuck a flower behind my ear somedays
we can watch entire seasons of all your favourite sitcoms on the holidays,
on the rare times that i write,
i wish my words,to be for your eyes
i might write about the silence we share
and the silly moments when you laugh at my jokes
i might write about how i haven't seen the moon since yesterday
and how i sometimes see it in mirror selfies of a certain office
i will write as you be,
because loving you is easy.
Happy birthday my love
